MyCOA Paywall — Post-Wizard Plan Selection

Paywall rendered after the onboarding wizard; filters plans by the company's current product count. Three scenarios shown side-by-side.

Ticket Scope

QP-3733 introduces the ordering paywall immediately after the onboarding wizard. The paywall surfaces three MyCOA plan tiers based on product count caps:

  • MyCOA 50 — up to 50 products — $249/mo
  • MyCOA 100 — up to 100 products — $399/mo
  • MyCOA Enterprise — up to 500 products — $499/mo

Plans below the company's current product count are filtered out. If product count exceeds all caps, a Contact Sales state replaces the plan cards. Payment happens on the next step; the in-app experience unlocks immediately after.

Filter Rules
Applied at paywall render — product count snapshot taken when the wizard completes.
Rule 1 — Hide below-cap plans

For every plan where products > plan.cap, mark the card disabled / filtered. Grey out the CTA and show a short reason.

Rule 2 — Promote minimum-eligible

The lowest-tier plan the catalog still fits becomes the highlighted recommendation (navy border + wash).

Rule 3 — Over-cap fallback

When products > Enterprise.cap (500), replace all plan cards with the Contact Sales card and surface catalog metrics for the AE.
