What Q-Config controls

Populates the fixed layout

Q-CONFIGPer-plan content

For each MyCOA subscription plan record, Q-Config supplies:

  • Plan name (rendered at card top)
  • Monthly price + yearly price
  • Product-count cap (used for filtering — see rules)
  • Feature bullets (the green-check rows)
  • MyCOA feature toggle (whether plan appears in MyCOA paywall at all)

Ordering & filter rules

QP-3733 scope What the ticket actually changes

Rule 1 — MyCOA toggle

Only plans where the Q-Config MyCOA feature toggle is on appear in this paywall. Plans flagged for other surfaces (Q-Trust-only, etc.) are excluded.

Rule 2 — Product-count cap

Plan is shown only if plan.cap ≥ company.product_count at wizard completion. Plans below the company's current catalog size are filtered out — the merchant can't downgrade under their own products.

Rule 3 — No plan fits

If every eligible plan is filtered out (catalog exceeds the largest cap), the paywall replaces the three cards with the Contact Sales fallback below. Never show an empty/broken paywall.
