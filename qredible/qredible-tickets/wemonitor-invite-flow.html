We Monitor · Invites tab — MyCOA feature toggle & activity dates

QP-3731 Invites tab gains a per-row Feature column (MyCOA toggle) and a stacked Activity Dates column (Sent / Joined / Last Active). License Status is removed. When MyCOA is toggled ON at invite-time, the recipient lands in the MyCOA setup wizard after joining, before the paywall.

ℹ DESIGN NOTES — NOT IN BUILD
  • Columns: checkbox · Legal Name · Email Invite Status · Company Status · Feature · Activity Dates · Team Members · Actions. License Status is removed. Inner Active/Pending sub-tabs are removed (Company Status pill carries that state inline).
  • Feature column = MyCOA toggle only. MyCOA is the only available feature at launch. Do not add Q-ScanAI, Policy Portal, or any other options here; plan→feature mapping lives in Q-Config (see QP-TBD — verify Jira key before merge).
  • MyCOA toggle behavior: ON → recipient is routed into the MyCOA setup wizard after joining and before the paywall. OFF → wizard is skipped. The inviter can flip the toggle at any time pre-join; after the invite is accepted the toggle is locked to whatever value was stamped on the token.
  • Activity Dates is a 3-line stack: Sent: MM/DD/YYYY · Joined: MM/DD/YYYY · Last Active: MM/DD/YYYY. Missing values render as —.
  • Email template modal is a single modal (not side-by-side default/override). A {{FEATURE_BLURB}} token in the body renders MyCOA copy when the per-invite toggle is ON, and a blank string when OFF.

QP-3731 · Recipient flow (MyCOA toggle ON)
What the invited merchant sees when MyCOA is ON
When MyCOA is toggled ON at invite-time:
  1. The recipient accepts the invite and completes account signup.
  2. They are routed directly into the MyCOA setup wizard — upload first COA, display preferences, domain/CNAME intent.
  3. On wizard completion they hit the paywall, filtered to MyCOA-eligible plans (plan→feature mapping lives in Q-Config, ticket QP-TBD — verify before merge).
  4. Plan selection finalizes the MyCOA subscription; the published MyCOA page goes live.
