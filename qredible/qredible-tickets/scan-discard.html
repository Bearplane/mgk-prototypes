Problem

When Zyte returns 0 products or a partial list, the system marks existing products as Removed — contaminating the audit trail with false removals. No safeguard exists to distinguish a failed scan from a legitimately empty catalog.

Phase 1 — Zero Guard (BR-1–3)
BR-1If scan returns 0 products, do not update catalog or scan change column.
BR-2Surface warning banner: "Scan returned 0 products — no changes committed." with Re-trigger option.
BR-3Record scan in history as Failed — No Products Returned, distinct from a successful scan.
Phase 2 — Discard Action (BR-4–8)
BR-4Admin-only "Discard Scan" button visible in the scan strip next to Last Scan metadata.
BR-5Confirmation modal shows scan date, products affected, and what will/won't be rolled back before executing.
BR-6Discard rolls back scan change column for affected products to pre-scan state. Does not remove ingested COAs or products.
BR-7Discarded scan record is removed from scan history.
BR-8Discard action logged in audit trail with user and timestamp.
Acceptance Criteria
☐0-product scan triggers warning banner, no catalog changes
☐Failed scan recorded as Failed — No Products Returned
☐Discard Scan button visible in scan strip for admins
☐Confirmation modal shows correct scan metadata before executing
☐After discard: products return to pre-scan change status
☐Ingested COAs and products not removed by discard
☐Discard logged in audit trail
