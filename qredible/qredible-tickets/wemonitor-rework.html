Pending tab = Underwriting Queue. Companies start here when imported or added manually. Analysts complete required scans (Website, Product, License Check), then promote the company to Active when ready for ongoing monitoring. Bulk "Run All Scans" kicks off all three scan types for selected companies simultaneously.

💡 Industry Type + Type of Company display: Shown as paired pills with a simple separator dot (e.g., Hemp / CBD·Retailer). These appear as badges under the company's Legal Name — NOT a separate column. Each IT+ToC pair is one connected visual unit. Multi-industry companies show multiple pairs. Filtering works on either dimension independently.

Feature Comparison — Before vs. After

Feature Before After
Industry/type pair filtering ❌ Not available ✅ Search/filter by industry type, type of company, or their paired combination
Pending tab ❌ Active / Archived / Invites only ✅ Underwriting queue with scan status

Select rows ❌ Not available ✅ Checkbox + select all + bulk toolbar
Multi-industry companies ❌ Single flat industry pill only ✅ Multiple paired pills under Legal Name, one pair per Industry Type + Type of Company combination
