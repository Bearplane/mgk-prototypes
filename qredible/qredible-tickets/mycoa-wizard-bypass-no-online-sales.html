Merchant Record — State After Bypass

When a merchant clicks "I do not sell online", two flags are written to their record. This panel documents the before/after state and downstream effects for engineering and product review.

MyCOA page published without "Resume Shopping" button
When no_online_sales = true and resume_shopping_url = null, the MyCOA template suppresses the hero CTA. The page still shows product search, COA explainer, and footer — but no outbound shopping link.
Wizard will not re-prompt for a shopping URL on future logins
Once mycoa_wizard_bypass = "no_online_sales" is set, the first-run gate checks this flag and skips directly to the Paywall (if paywall_status = pending) or the MyCOA dashboard (if active). The merchant won't see the URL prompt again unless they manually add one from Settings.
Payment processor cases remain fully functional
Case management, COA uploads, and product assignment are not gated on resume_shopping_url. A merchant can receive and respond to cases from payment processors normally — the bypass only affects the wizard flow and the storefront CTA on their public MyCOA page.
