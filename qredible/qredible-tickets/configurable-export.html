Persistence Behavior
ℹ️
Per-user, per-tab persistence

Last-used preset is saved in localStorage keyed by userId + tabName.

Presets themselves are stored server-side (user account). Switching users or tabs resets to that context's last-used preset.

Products tab remembers its own preset independently of the COAs tab.
