Persistence: Export settings are stored per user. The last-used preset is remembered across sessions. Quick Export (⌘E) uses the active preset without opening the modal — useful for recurring exports like weekly Flex Bank submissions. Preset names are shown on the button so users always know what they'll get before clicking.
