URL Configuration New

Every MyCOA page comes with two types of URLs — a listing URL (your full COA library with search) and direct COA links (unique URL for each individual COA). Both are always available. The choice below determines which domain they live on.

🌐
Default — Qredible Domain
Current Feature
Your MyCOA pages are hosted on the standard Qredible domain. No setup required — works out of the box.
Listing URL
members.qredible.com/my-coa/black-bear-hemp-dispensary-164911
Direct COA Link
members.qredible.com/my-coa/black-bear-hemp-dispensary-164911/coa/thca-live-rosin-sex-panther/02-18-2025-5853
Pros: Zero setup. Instant. QR codes and direct links work immediately.
✨
Custom Domain — SEO Optimized
Recommended
Point your own subdomain to MyCOA via CNAME. Same content, your brand's domain. Better for SEO — search engines attribute authority to your domain.
Listing URL
coa.blackbeardispensary.com
Direct COA Link
coa.blackbeardispensary.com/thca-live-rosin-sex-panther/02-18-2025-5853
Pros: Brand authority. SEO juice goes to your domain. Professional appearance. Custom SSL.
Always Available (Both Options)
