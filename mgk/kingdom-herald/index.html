§

4

5
Director (5) — hero management, guild ops, officer missions
§
Weekly Vault Mechanics
6
Vault resets every 7 epochs — epoch % 7 == 0 triggers GoalManager.HandleWeeklyChest()
7
Each GoalCategory has 5 tiers. Token earned per tier: TokenAmountForGoal = currentTier > 0 ? 1 : 0
8
Bonus token: +1 if ALL 5 categories have currentTier ≥ 1 (max weekly = 26 tokens)
9
Tokens accumulate in VaultManager._vaultTokens. Spent on WeeklyVault rewards
10
VaultManager keeps last 3 weekly vaults in availableVaults list
§
Vault Reward Types by Category
11
Combatant — equippable gear items (level-scaled, tiers 1–5 progressively better)
12
Ambassador — merchant rep, town rep, selected-town combo at tier 5
13
Craftsman — GuildMaterial lootboxes, Scroll items (crafting patterns)
14
Director — Invitations, gear, Gold lootbox, StarCoin bundle
15
Mercantile — Gold lootboxes of increasing scale (tier 1-5)
§
Goal Tier Progression
16
Goals advance via GoalManager.AdvanceGoal(float, GoalCategory) — activity points accumulate
17
Tier up fires Goal.OnTierChange + VaultManager.TriggerAlterToken() + reward claim
18
Tier 5 = goal complete for the week. Tier 5 reward: Item #1022018 (shared across categories)
